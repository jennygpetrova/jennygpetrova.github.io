Modularity Based Algorithms for Image Segmentation

Research on community detection algorithms using graph-based modularity optimization with applications to image segmentation and network partitioning problems.

Available materials: Research Materials

2023–2024 Development period

Overview

This research explores algorithms for community detection in networks using modularity optimization. The focus is on efficient approaches for identifying cohesive substructures in complex graphs, with particular emphasis on applications to image segmentation problems.

The research covers both theoretical foundations and practical implementations, addressing computational efficiency and scalability for large-scale graph partitioning problems.

Research Materials

Key papers and resources:

    Community Detection Algorithms — Analysis of modularity-based detection methods
  • Fast Graph-Based Algorithms — Efficient computational approaches for large graphs

Methods and Techniques

Core methodologies include spectral clustering approaches, modularity-driven optimization algorithms, and graph partitioning techniques. Implementation focuses on scalable solutions for computational efficiency across varying graph sizes and density distributions.
